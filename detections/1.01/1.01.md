# 1.01 - Login from a highly-priviledged account
## Description
A login occured from a highly priviledged account (e.g. Super Admin, Org Admin) to Google Cloud,
be it from Cloud Console, Workspace Admin Console or gcloud CLI. 


**Use Cases:** Detect</br>
